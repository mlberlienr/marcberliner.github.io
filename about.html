About Me

I'm Marc Berliner, a Product Designer based in Denver, Colorado. I studied Industrial and Interactive Design at Syracuse University, where I built a strong foundation in human-centered design that continues to shape my work today.

Over the past several years, I've focused on designing digital experiences for healthcare organizations. I've partnered with hospitals and academic medical centers to improve experiences for patients, providers, caregivers, researchers, and employees. My work spans product strategy, user research, UX design, design systems, and accessibility, with a focus on creating intuitive, inclusive experiences that solve real user problems.

Before moving into digital product design, I designed consumer products and packaging for beauty brands, where I learned to balance aesthetics with functionality. I also worked in behavioral healthcare, supporting patients receiving treatment for addiction and mental health conditions. That experience strengthened my empathy and continues to influence how I approach research, collaboration, and design.

Outside of work, I enjoy skiing, hiking, working out, and exploring the fine arts through drawing, painting, and sculpting.
